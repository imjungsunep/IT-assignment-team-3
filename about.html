About TechNova Electronics

Company History

TechNova Electronics began in 2018 as a small campus-focused retailer of laptops and accessories. Over the years we expanded our catalog to include audio gear, peripherals, and smart devices while keeping student-friendly prices and honest advice at the center of what we do.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To make reliable technology accessible through clear information, fair pricing, and responsive support.

Vision: To become the region’s most trusted electronics partner for education and small business, known for integrity and lasting customer relationships.

Services Offered

  • Retail sales of laptops, accessories, and peripherals
  • Procurement and bulk orders for institutions
  • Product consultation and after-sales guidance
  • Warranty coordination with manufacturers
